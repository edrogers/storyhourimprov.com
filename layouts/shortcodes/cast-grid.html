Meet Our Cast

Emma Reynolds

Emma brings vibrant energy to every performance with her background in musical theater and improv comedy. A founding member of Story Hour, she's known for her quick wit and remarkable vocal range.

Sophia Washington

With a background in children's theater and education, Sophia creates characters that resonate with kids. Her specialty is transforming audience suggestions into memorable songs that have everyone singing along.

Marcus Chen

Marcus's background in puppetry and physical comedy makes him a favorite with the younger crowd. His animated performances and talent for creating distinct character voices bring each story to life.

Olivia Patel

Olivia combines her vocal jazz training with a natural storytelling ability. As our musical director, she seamlessly weaves together the cast's diverse talents into harmonious, spontaneous musical numbers.

Jackson Taylor

Jackson blends classical piano training with a passion for storytelling. His ability to compose melodies on the fly adds a magical dimension to each show, captivating young audiences everywhere.

Noah Rodriguez

Noah's improvisational skills and background in children's literature help shape each performance. His knack for creating relatable characters ensures every show connects with both kids and parents alike.
